# About Checkpoint

Checkpoint is a decentralized protocol that bridges off-chain loyalty points and rewards programs with on-chain liquidity. We enable seamless tokenization, trading, and settlement of points before Token Generation Events (TGE), making points liquid and tradeable based on your efforts and merits.

## The Problem We Solve

### Current State of Points

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fragmented Systems">
    Points exist in isolated silos across different protocols and platforms
  </Step>
  <Step title="No Liquidity">
    Points have no inherent value or trading mechanism until official token launches
  </Step>
  <Step title="Uncertain Value">
    Users cannot monetize their engagement or predict future token value
  </Step>
  <Step title="Limited Access">
    Only early adopters or large holders benefit from points accumulation
  </Step>
</Steps>
